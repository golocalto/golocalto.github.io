Toronto's Live Music Scene

Discover the sounds of our city; Listen live, go local

There are so many different melodies drifting in and out of Toronto, from international treasures to homegrown talent, our city hosts a wide variety of amazing artists. Jump into the rabbit hole that is Toronto's music scene. We cover intimate local concerts; browse original photography, reviews, recomendations, and see what shows are on our radar.

About

Go Local, Toronto aims to help promote local musical talent as well as emerging artists passing through the city. We also hope to help listeners discover new, fantastic sounds that otherwise may have gone unheard. We offer photography services to artists and provide updates to music fanatics on upcoming shows, spectaclular acts, and exciting new music releases. Toronto's music scene is growing rapidly; don't miss out.
